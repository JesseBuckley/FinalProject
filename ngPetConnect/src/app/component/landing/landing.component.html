Welcome to Pet Connect!


What We Do

Our app serves as a dynamic platform where like-minded individuals come together to connect, share experiences, and pool valuable resources!


About

Conceived as the culmination of our final project for Skill Distillery, this application is the result of our collective passion for pets and the desire to create a unique space for pet owners to connect and thrive.
Throughout the development process, we poured in countless hours of hard work, dedication, and creative energy to bring this vision to life. From conceptualization to execution, we meticulously crafted each feature with the aim of fostering a sense of community among pet owners.
